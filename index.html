about me

Hey there, my name is Teodor Garzdin.

I'm not normal. Not in any sense of the word. I go out of my comfort zone way more then I should and I'm not your boring avareage guy. I love to travel, I love life and I love the sun! I love portraying the world trough my eyes. That's why I love photography. I got my first camera when I was 7 and since then I've fallen in love with the magical piece of technology that lets you capture beautiful sunsets and lovely smiles.

I have an eye for the beautiful. Beauty is all around us, in the morning when the steaming coffee hits your lips or in the autumn when leaves are falling.

I love design and technology. I love how technology empowers us to do things that were impossible a decade ago.

I love beautiful simplistic design. Everything with me is about simplicity.

That was completely honest and now you know me as much as I know myself. So if you would like to get in touch just use the contact form below.
